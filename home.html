MELC Genomics is a framework for DNA assembly.

The development of the NGS techniques made possible the assembly of complete genomes as well as several analysis, contributing to advances in many areas such as human health, animal production and ecology.

Nevertheless, due to the fact that most of organisms do not have a reference genome, strategies for de novo assembly were developed. The most common strategies are based on graphs, such as De Bruijn Graphs and Overlap Layout Consensus (OLC).

Here we made available a complete pipeline for de novo genome assembly, where the user will be able to:

  • system information and performance monitoring;

  • check the quality before and after the processing of the raw data files;

  • assemble the genome;

  • check the quality of the assembly.
